Division of Water Resources Services

 

Dam Safety

 

Dam Safety Branch has responsibility for developing and executing a comprehensive dam safety risk management program for all dams in the state of Colorado in the interest of public safety and sustainable and resilient water storage.

 

Learn More

 

Data & Information

 

The Data & Information Section provides free public access to a variety of water research tools, including tabular data, map viewer and imaged documents.

 

 

Learn More

 

Water Administration

 

The Water Administration Sections, led by Division Engineers in 7 Statewide Division Offices, administer the waters of the state and Colorado's water sharing agreements with other states.

 

 

Learn More

 

Well Construction & Inspection

 

The Well Construction and Inspection Sections collect and publish hydrogeologic data, work with the Board of Examiners, and enforce well construction standards to protect public health.

 

Learn More

 

Well Metering

 

The Well Metering Section oversees compliance with State Engineer Use and Measurement Rules within the Republican, South Platte, Arkansas and Rio Grande basins.

 

 

Learn More

 

Well Permitting

 

 The Well Permitting Section evaluates applications for well permits throughout the state, including Denver Basin and Designated Basin areas.  

 

 

Learn More
